The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. The Last Samurai

This is my favorite Hollywood movie by Tom Cruise depicting the ancient warriors of Japan - the samurai.

2. Inu Yasha : The Castle Through the Looking Mirror

This is the first anime movie I watched about medieval Japan.

3. The Lion King

This is the first Walt Disney I've watched, with good graphics and art.
